I'm an aerospace engineering graduate student (he/him) currently based in India Blacksburg, Virginia. I'm pursuing my MS at Virginia Tech, where I work on solid-fuel ramjet combustion and CFD modeling at the Advanced Propulsion and Power Laboratory.

I'm deeply interested in computational fluid dynamics, hypersonic propulsion, and motorsport engineering. When I'm not running simulations, I'm analyzing F1 telemetry data or dreaming about careers in Formula 1.
